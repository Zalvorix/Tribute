Revan the Prodigal Knight

Conqueror Savior Battlefield Genius

Summary

Revan was a human man who was born around 3994 BBY in the Outer Rim. He was found by the Jedi and was instrumental in defeating the Mandalorians. He and his friend Alek latter left for the Unknown Regions and returned as Sith. After being shot by Malak he was believed to have died. However, he was taken by the Jedi Council and reprogrammed with a new identity after his mind was destroyed.
